🏫 About Sri Barathi Vidyalaya

Building future-ready students through quality education, values, and innovation.

📖 About the School

The School Management is committed to fulfilling all the requirements necessary for the development of the school and its students. A team of highly qualified, experienced, and talented teaching faculty helps us achieve our educational goals.

Our campus provides modern infrastructure with spacious, well-ventilated classrooms and a healthy learning environment. Students become part of a dynamic system where they actively explore, learn continuously, and take responsibility for their education.

In today’s interconnected world, we focus on global-minded learning. Our approach combines personalized education and reflective practices to help students develop academic excellence along with essential life skills.

🏛️ Foundation & History

Sri Barathi Vidyalaya Trust was established in Mannargudi with the noble objective of serving social and cultural needs. The trustees include Sri C. Krishnan, K. Raghavan, and Srimathi Booma Krishnan, who also serves as the Correspondent.

The school was founded on 2nd June 1997 as a Nursery and Primary School with English as the medium of instruction. Recognition was granted by the Government in the same year.

In 1998, the High School section was started, and classes from VI to IX were introduced. The first batch appeared for the Government X Standard Examination in 2002. Over the years, the institution has grown into a full-fledged school offering both English and Tamil medium education.

🎯 Vision & Mission

👁️ Vision

Education is not just about knowledge, but about nurturing curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking. We aim to create a holistic learning environment that encourages lifelong learning, inclusivity, and adaptability.

🎓 Mission

Our mission is to empower every learner to reach their full potential through quality and inclusive education. We focus on building strong academic foundations, critical thinking, and leadership skills to prepare students for a changing world.

💎 Values

  • Develop students’ skills and refine their social behavior
  • Encourage interest in General Knowledge and creativity
  • Promote handwriting excellence and discipline
  • Conduct activities like GK tests and drawing competitions
  • Provide holistic education including Physical Education and Computer Science
  • Support learning with trained and dedicated teachers

Our values focus on building well-rounded individuals with knowledge, discipline, and creativity.
