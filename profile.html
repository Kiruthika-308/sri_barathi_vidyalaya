About

The School Management is committed to fulfill all the requirements necessary for the development of the school and its Students. A term of highly qualified, experienced and talented teaching faculty helps us to achieve the aims of the school. The school campus has necessary infrastructure with modern, spacious, well ventilated and healthy environment. Your child will become a part of a dynamic learning environment where they can actively inquire, continually build on their understanding of the world, and take responsibility for their learning.

We all live in an interconnected world with a growing focus on becoming more globally minded and SBV educates children for the future. Our approach integrates personalised learning and reflective practices that enable our students not only to achieve academic results but also to develop the right skill sets to thrive in this ever-changing world. With the strong humanitarian values, a rock-solid foundation from early years through the pre-university program – opens the doors to every child’s unquenchable ambition to denote and connote the existence of anything.

Sri Barathi Vidyalaya School

SBV School, raised on the lofty ideals of the Founder Trustee Mr.C.Krishnan & Mrs.K.Booma krishnan, is widely acknowledged as one of the leading institutions among the schools in Mannargudi located in the heart of the city.
