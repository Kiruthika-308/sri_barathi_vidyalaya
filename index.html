About

SBV School, raised on the lofty ideals of the Founder Trustee Mr.C.Krishnan & Mrs.K.Booma krishnan, is widely acknowledged as one of the leading institutions among the schools in Mannargudi located in the heart of the city.

Our Teaching Philosophy :
At Sri Barathi Vidyalaya, our teachers are more than just educators—they are mentors, facilitators, and lifelong learners committed to nurturing the potential of every student.

Beyond Classroom:
It is essential to develop our young minds’ thinking ability, innovativeness & the ability to function properly in society, hence we offer several co-curricular activities and extracurricular activities such as Silambam, Karate, Archery, Chess

Excellent Transportation:
Our school provides top-notch transportation facilities for students residing in and around our town. Whether you're in the heart of the city or nestled in the outskirts, we've got you covered!
