About the School

The School Management is committed to fulfilling all requirements for the development of the school and its students. With highly qualified and experienced teachers, we ensure quality education in a modern, well-ventilated and healthy environment.

🌍

Global Learning

We prepare students for an interconnected world by integrating personalized learning and reflective practices, helping them build knowledge, skills, and confidence.

🎓

Student Growth

Students become part of a dynamic learning environment where they explore, grow, and take responsibility for their learning journey.

Foundation & History

📜

Establishment

Sri Barathi Vidyalaya Trust was established in Mannargudi with a vision to serve society through education.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Leadership

Trustees include Sri C. Krishnan, K. Raghavan, and Srimathi Booma Krishnan (Correspondent).

🏫

School Growth

Started in 1997 as a primary school, later expanded to high school with both English and Tamil medium.
